Fren Cat Game Loop

FREN runs an active cat-based gameplay system with progression and economy mechanics.

  • Core flow: `game start` -> `discover/scavenge` -> `shelter/habitat` -> `rescue/merge/sell`.
  • Collection-oriented progression with cats, materials, and strategy choices.
  • Leaderboards and stats via `fren game lb` and `fren game stats`.
  • Built for daily gameplay loops and long-term retention.
Puzzle & Word Games

Multiple fast-turn games run directly in channels with individual command groups.

  • `fren sudoku` with difficulty/mode options, hints, and leaderboards.
  • `fren hangman`, `fren scramble`, and `fren guessnumber` for casual play.
  • `fren gtw` (Guess The Word) with hint support and closeness reactions.
Social & Party Games

FREN also supports social game formats for group participation and events.

  • `fren story` collaborative storytelling with channel history.
  • `fren truthordare` with lobbies, scoring, and leaderboards.
  • `fren ematch` real-time emoji match challenges between members.
AI Study Buddy & Chat

Guided help for learning-focused conversations and fast query support inside Discord.

Moderation & Safety

Tools to keep communities healthy while reducing repetitive admin work.

Reminders & Tracking

Scheduling and reminder features for day-to-day community operations.
